Frequently Asked

How long does it take to get to Benguet from Manila?

Approximately 6-7 hours by bus or private vehicle, depending on traffic and route.

What's the best time to visit?

November to February for cool, dry weather and the famous sea of clouds.

Do I need a guide?

While not required for most areas, a local guide is highly recommended for mountain treks.
