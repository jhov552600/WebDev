Things to Do in Benguet

From adventurous treks to cultural experiences, Benguet offers activities for every type of traveler

Mountain Trekking

Conquer Mount Pulag, the third highest peak in the Philippines, or explore numerous trails through pine forests and grasslands.

Difficulty: Moderate to Challenging
Duration: Half-day to 2 days
Photography Tours

Capture stunning landscapes, rice terraces, flower farms, and the famous sea of clouds at sunrise.

Difficulty: Easy
Duration: 2-4 hours
Food Tasting

Sample fresh strawberries, local vegetables, Cordillera coffee, and traditional Igorot cuisine.

Difficulty: Easy
Duration: 1-3 hours
Market Shopping

Browse local markets for fresh produce, handwoven textiles, woodcarvings, and unique Cordillera crafts.

Difficulty: Easy
Duration: 2-3 hours
Mountain Biking

Ride through scenic mountain roads and challenging trails with breathtaking views of terraced landscapes.

Difficulty: Moderate to Challenging
Duration: 3-5 hours
Strawberry Picking

Visit famous strawberry farms in La Trinidad and pick your own fresh strawberries straight from the garden.

Difficulty: Easy
Duration: 1-2 hours
Nature Walks

Stroll through pine tree parks, botanical gardens, and flower farms while enjoying the cool mountain breeze.

Difficulty: Easy
Duration: 1-3 hours
Cultural Immersion

Experience indigenous Igorot culture, visit traditional villages, and learn about ancient customs and rituals.

Difficulty: Easy
Duration: Half-day to full day

Seasonal Events & Festivals

February
Panagbenga Festival

Baguio's flower festival featuring grand floats, street dancing, and vibrant blooms.

March
Strawberry Festival

La Trinidad's celebration of strawberry harvest with activities and local products.

November-December
Adivay Festival

Provincial thanksgiving festival showcasing Benguet's rich cultural heritage.

Activity Tips

What to Bring

  • Warm clothing (temperatures can drop to 10°C)
  • Comfortable hiking shoes for treks
  • Camera for capturing stunning views
  • Sunscreen and hat for daytime activities
  • Reusable water bottle to stay hydrated

Important Reminders

  • Book Mount Pulag treks in advance through DENR
  • Respect indigenous culture and traditions
  • Practice Leave No Trace principles
  • Hire local guides for better experiences
  • Check weather conditions before outdoor activities
