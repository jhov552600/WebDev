Discover Benguet's Destinations

From vibrant cities to serene mountain villages, explore the diverse landscapes and cultural treasures of Benguet Province

Highlights

La Trinidad

Highlights
Strawberry Farms Vegetable Terraces Flower Farms

Best time: December to March

Tuba

Home to scenic rice terraces, waterfalls, and authentic Cordillera cultural experiences.

Highlights
Rice Terraces Twin Peaks Asin Hot Springs

Kabayan

Ancient burial grounds, mummy caves, and stunning mountain landscapes await adventurers.

Highlights
Fire Mummies Mount Pulag Traditional Villages

Best time: December to May

Atok

The vegetable bowl of Benguet, featuring endless gardens and cool mountain air.

Highlights
Vegetable Terraces Flower Gardens Sunrise Views

Planning Your Visit

Getting There

Benguet is accessible via the Kennon Road, Marcos Highway, or Halsema Highway from Manila

Duration

Plan for at least 3-5 days to fully experience the main attractions

Best Season

Visit during the dry season (November to April) for the best weather
